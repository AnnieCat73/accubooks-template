Cookie Policy for Accubooks

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and to personalize the content and advertisements we show you.

A cookie is a small text file that is stored on your device when you visit our website. These text files contain information that can later be read by the website that generated them.

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

  1. Functional cookies: These cookies are essential for the proper functioning of our website and allow you to navigate and use its features.
  2. Analytical cookies: These cookies collect information about how you use our website, such as which pages you visit and how often. This helps us understand and improve our website's performance.
  3. Advertising cookies: These cookies are used to show you relevant advertisements on our website and other websites you visit.

You can control and manage cookies by adjusting the settings on your browser. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website and limit your ability to access certain features.

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

If you have any questions or concerns about our cookie policy, please contact us.
